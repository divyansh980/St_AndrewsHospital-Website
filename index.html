Welcome to St. Andrews Hospital - Your Trusted Healthcare Partner

At St. Andrews Hospital, we are committed to providing top-notch healthcare services with a focus on your well-being. part of our dedication to comprehensive care,
we are pleased to offer a range of Medicare plan options tailored to meet your unique needs.

  • Medicare Advantage Plans:

    Experience the convenience of all-in-one coverage with our Medicare Advantage plans. These plans often include Medicare Part A and Part B, as well as additional benefits such as prescription drug coverage and wellness programs.

  • Medicare Supplement Insurance (Medigap):

    Supplement your Original Medicare coverage with our Medigap plans. These plans help cover the "gaps" in your Medicare coverage, including copayments, deductibles, and coinsurance, providing you with enhanced financial protection.

  • Prescription Drug Plans (Part D):

    Ensure access to the medications you need with our Medicare Part D prescription drug plans. Our plans are designed to help you manage prescription costs while maintaining the quality of care you deserve.

Why Choose St. Andrews Hospital for Your Medicare Needs?

    Our commitment to patient-centered care ensures that your health and well-being are at the forefront of our services. We prioritize open communication, respect, and personalized attention.

  • Experienced Healthcare Professionals:

    Benefit from the expertise of our skilled healthcare professionals who are dedicated to providing compassionate and quality care. Your health is in capable hands at St. Andrews Hospital.

  • Convenient Access:

    With strategically located facilities and a network of healthcare providers, accessing our services is convenient and hassle-free. We aim to make healthcare accessible to all members of our community.
